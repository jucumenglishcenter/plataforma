Two template types, exactly as you described:

• Reading Comprehension = level-variant. The step structure itself changes: 5 L1-scaffolded steps at Pre-A1 → 3 reduced steps at A1 → a minimal no-Spanish sequence at A2. On top of that, scaffolding intensity fades by week within each level (a progression dynamic, not per-story).

• Grammar Review (the 5-step Summary) = level-invariant. Always 5 steps at every level — only the content changes. Spanish is part of the steps, not a scaffold that gets removed (at A2, Step 3 shifts to L2-internal differences).

▶ How to use: pick a Level (Pre-A1 / A1 / A2), then drag the Week slider. Watch the left card (Reading) change its steps and Spanish support; the right card (Grammar Review) stays fixed at 5 steps. The reading passage below is live — the translation is shown, one-click, or hidden depending on the scaffolding tier.
